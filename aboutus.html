    About Us

    Protecting your identity is our mission. The rise in ID fraud across Africa is alarming and South Africa is listed number 1 on the fast growing ID fraud related cases in the continent. Discover how MyID safeguards you from identity fraud.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to partner with retail shops, insurance service providers, banks, and loan providers to help secure our clients identity information and prevent unauthorized access, theft or fraud.

    Our Vision

    We envision a future where every individual is fully protected from identity fraud, with easy-to-use tools to track and control the usage of their ID across multiple industries.

    Who We Are

    myID is a leading identity protection platform designed to help individuals and businesses prevent identity fraud. Our goal is to make sure ID theft becomes a thing of the past by monitoring and managing your ID's usage across various sectors, including retail, banks, loans, and more.

    Why Choose MyID?

    • Real-time ID monitoring across retail, banking, and loan sectors.
    • Alerts when your ID is used for loans, contracts, or even marriage registrations.
    • Approve legitimate transactions or flag fraudulent ones with ease.
